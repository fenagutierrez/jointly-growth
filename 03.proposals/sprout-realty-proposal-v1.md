# Proposal: Sprout Realty x Jointly Implementation Plan

## Executive Summary
This proposal outlines a "Zero-Friction" transition for Sprout Realty to Jointly. We recognize that timing and bandwidth are the primary blockers for Cody and the leadership team. To address this, Jointly will take on the heavy lifting of the migration, providing a "White Glove" implementation that minimizes the administrative load on Sprout.

## The "White Glove" Transition Plan
To ensure a seamless move from Dotloop, Jointly will provide the following:

### 1. Dedicated Migration Specialist
We will assign a dedicated Jointly team member to manually migrate active files and templates from Dotloop.
*   **Template Mapping:** We will recreate your existing Dotloop loops/templates in Jointly, ensuring your custom compliance workflows are preserved.
*   **Manual File Transfer:** For active transactions, we will assist in transitioning documentation so no data is lost during the move.

### 2. Admin & Compliance Sync
We will work directly with your admin/compliance staff (if available) to set up:
*   **Office-Level Controls:** Reviewing and configuring approval workflows.
*   **Broker Visibility:** Ensuring Cody has full oversight without needing to manage the day-to-day setup.

### 3. Progressive Training Rollout
Instead of a single overwhelming session, we will deliver a tiered training program:
*   **Phase 1: Admin/Leadership Deep-Dive** (1 hour) – Focus on oversight and configuration.
*   **Phase 2: Agent Success Series** (3-4 sessions) – Hands-on workshops tailored to the agents' Experience.
*   **Phase 3: Ongoing Office Hours** – Weekly 30-minute drop-in sessions for the first month to ensure adoption.

## Pricing & Value
Sprout Realty is currently paying more for Dotloop than the Jointly "Business Edition" cost.

*   **Platform Subscription:** All-in price of **$1,539/month** (covers up to ~95 users).
*   **Implementation Fee:** **WAIVED** ($1,500 value) as part of this partnership launch.

## Implementation Roadmap & Rollout Strategy

### Phase 1: Power User Onboarding (2-3 Weeks)
We begin with a targeted soft launch to stress-test the custom workflows and migration before the full office move.
*   **Participants:** 10 Power Users, including at least **2 core administrative staff members** and **8 high-volume agents**.
*   **Goal:** Validate template accuracy, refine compliance approval paths, and identify office-specific "frequently asked questions."
*   **Feedback Loop:** A weekly 15-minute sync with the onboarding group to ensure the system is tuned to Sprout’s exact needs.

### Phase 2: Full Office Transition (Immediate Success)
Immediately following the successful completion of the Power User Onboarding Phase, we will move the remaining 85+ users into the platform.
*   **Bulk Onboarding:** All remaining users are added simultaneously.
*   **Agent Success Series:** We will execute the 3-4 session training series, starting with an office-wide Kick-off event to demonstrate the new, simplified workflow.
*   **Legacy Data:** Users can continue to access Dotloop for historical records, while all new transactions are initiated in Jointly.

### Phase 3: Stabilization & Adoption
*   **Ongoing Support:** Weekly "Office Hours" and dedicated chat support for all Sprout users.
*   **Adoption Audit:** A 30-day review with Cody to confirm that the team is successfully using the platform and identify any further optimization opportunities.
