# Proposal: Central Metro Realty x Jointly Partnership

## Executive Summary
Central Metro Realty is a high-volume brokerage with a transactional model that prioritizes agent independence and low overhead. This proposal introduces a custom **$3/seat "Starter Tier"** designed to provide a "Zero-Cost" experience for agents while equipping the brokerage with professional-grade compliance and back-office tools.

## The Solution: Brokerage-Wide Efficiency
To meet Central Metro's unique economic requirements, we are proposing a hybrid feature set that maximizes value where it matters most: compliance and transaction management.

### 1. For the Agents: "Unlimited Starter" Experience
Agents gain access to Jointly’s streamlined transaction platform at no individual cost, replacing their need for fragmented third-party tools.
*   **Starter Feature Set:** Access to Jointly’s core transaction management interface.
*   **Unlimited Transactions:** Included in the brokerage package (regularly a **$9/month** individual agent value).
*   **TREC & TR Forms Integration:** Access to the full library of TREC and Texas REALTORS® (TR) forms, supporting both Realtor and non-Realtor agent requirements.
*   **Electronic Signatures:** Seamless, integrated signing experience directly within the Jointly platform, eliminating the need for external signature tools.

### 2. For the Back Office: Full Command & Control
Central Metro’s leadership and compliance teams receive the full suite of "Business Edition" features to maintain oversight of 1,000+ agents.
*   **Compliance Review:** Tools to receive, review, and approve transaction documents.
*   **CDA Generation:** Streamlined generation of Commission Disbursement Authorizations.
*   **Status Tracking:** Real-time visibility into the entire brokerage pipeline.
*   **Custom Form Integration:** Ability to integrate Central Metro's specific internal forms and business rules.
