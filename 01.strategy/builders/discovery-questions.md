# Builder Segment: Discovery Questions

This document contains high-level discovery questions designed to assess the opportunity within the builder segment. These questions focus on pain points related to custom contracts and team collaboration.

## 1. Contract Workflow & "Builder Contract"
*   What percentage of your contracts are on your own proprietary "builder contract" versus standard association forms?
*   How do you currently generate these contract (technology)?
*   How much time is spent manually fixing contract errors or missing info?
*   Once a contract is signed, how do you extract the key terms into your back-office or accounting systems?
*   How do you currently audit your files to ensure 100% of disclosures and insurance requirements are met before a keys-in-hand closing?

## 2. Internal Sales & Team Visibility
*   Once an interested buyer is ready to move forward with a sales rep, what is the exact step-by-step process to get a contract in front of them?
*   Does the sales rep have the authority to generate and send the contract immediately, or is there a management/legal approval gate required first?
*   How do you currently track the real-time status of every deal across all your sales reps? Do you have a "single source of truth"?
*   If you were to double your production next year, which part of your current administrative process would break first?

## 3. Buyer & External Agent Collaboration
*   What is the typical split of your deals—what percentage is handled by your internal sales team versus those involving an external buyer agent?
*   How do buyers or external agents currently submit offers or "intent to purchase" to your sales reps?
*   How do you keep both the buyer and their agent informed of the process milestones?
*   What is the biggest friction point in communicating with buyers?

## 4. Inventory & Tech Stack
*   How do you sync your current inventory (plots, floor plans, specs) with the MLS? Is that a manual entry process?
*   What systems (CRM, ERP, Accounting) must this data flow into for a workflow to be considered "seamless"?
