## 2. ICP Detailed Profiles

### A. The Individual Agent (The "Bottom-Up" Entry)
*   **Profile:** Top-producing solo agent (10+ deals/year) in a tech-laggard brokerage.
*   **Pain Points:** 
    *   "E-signature fatigue" (paying for DocuSign + others).
    *   Messy client experience (emailing PDFs).
    *   Manual follow-ups taking time away from prospecting.
*   **The Jointly Hook:** "The 'TurboTax' experience for your clients. Look like a tech-powered powerhouse for $249/mo."
*   **Sales Motion:** Self-service demo → Automated trial → Conversion.

### B. The Team Lead (The "Mid-Market" Engine)
*   **Profile:** High-producing agent running a 5–15 person team. Often has a Transaction Coordinator (TC).
*   **Pain Points:**
    *   Team Lead spends 10+ hours/week auditing agent files.
    *   Inconsistent deal quality across the team.
    *   Paying for a "Tech Stack" that agents don't use.
*   **The Jointly Hook:** "Infrastructure over Splits. Scale your team's volume without scaling your admin time."
*   **Sales Motion:** 15-minute Speed Demo (focused on TC efficiency).

### C. The "Squeezed" Boutique Broker (Tier 2/3)
*   **Profile:** Owner-operated firm (1–50 agents). Local Texas brand.
*   **Pain Points:**
    *   Losing agents to 100% commission shops (e.g., eXp, Fathom).
    *   Manual TREC compliance audits are a "time-tax" on the Broker’s own production.
    *   Technology looks "small" compared to national competitors (Compass/Redfin).
*   **The Jointly Hook:** "National-Grade Infrastructure, Local-Brand Identity. Get a compliance department for $0 net cost via Rev-Share."
*   **Sales Motion:** AI-driven outreach (Katarina) + "Net-Zero" profitability pitch.

### D. The Enterprise Brokerage (Tier 1)
*   **Profile:** 50+ agents, multiple offices, $1K+ MRR potential.
*   **Pain Points:**
    *   Enterprise-wide compliance risk and liability.
    *   Fragmentation of data across offices.
    *   Need for a recruitment "Edge" to attract top-producing teams.
*   **The Jointly Hook:** "The Operating System for the Modern Brokerage. Standardize excellence across every office."
*   **Sales Motion:** High-touch, Devin-led executive sales.
