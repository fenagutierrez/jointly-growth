# GTM Strategy: Tier 3 Texas Brokerages (<15 Agents)

## 1. Executive Summary
Tier 3 brokerages represent the "Long Tail" of the real estate market in Texas. This segment is underserved by enterprise tools but too complex for simple e-signature utilities. Our strategy shifts from "Selling a Tool" to "Providing a Professional Infrastructure" (Business-in-a-Box).

**Goal:** 20 New Accounts/Month (Texas-wide)
**Target MRR:** $250 - $500 per account
**Total Monthly Target:** $5,000 - $10,000 New MRR

## 3. Implementation Plan (Texas-Wide Focus)

### Phase 1: Preparation (Active)
1.  **Texas Growth Scrapers:** Finalize TREC sponsorship delta report process.
2.  **Speed Demo Script:** Finalize the 15-minute flow using the **Rev-Share Calculator**.
3.  **Recruiting Asset:** Create the "Why Join [Brokerage Name]" slide deck template for new sign-ups.
4.  **Boutique Webflow Templates:** Finalize 3-5 high-conversion real estate templates with integrated CMS.
5.  **Media Partner Network:** Vet and onboard 2-3 regional media partners for the "Content Kickstart" one-time package.
6.  **Lead Gen R&D:** Investigate the dynamics and compliance rules for generating and distributing "Expired Listing" leads.



### Phase 2: Execution
1.  **Launch Texas "Katarina" Experiment:** Begin outreach to boutique brokers on Instagram/LinkedIn celebrating new hires.
2.  **Association Partnership Outreach:** Pitch smaller Texas AORs (Associations of Realtors) on the "Brokerage Starter Grant."
3.  **SEO Content Push:** Publish 3 articles on boutique brokerage operations in Texas.

## 4. Verification & KPIs
*   **Lead Velocity:** 15 Qualified Leads/Week (Texas-wide).
*   **CAC:** Keep under $500 (1-month payback on $500 MRR).
*   **Conversion Rate:** Aim for 33% from Demo to Onboarding.
