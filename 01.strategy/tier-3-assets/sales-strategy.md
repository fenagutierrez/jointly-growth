# GTM Asset: Sales Strategy (High-Velocity Tier 3 Texas)

## 1. High-Velocity Sales Model

| Feature | Traditional Sales | Jointly High-Velocity (Tier 3) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Sales Cycle** | 30–90 Days | 7–14 Days |

| **Primary Value** | "Features & Benefits" | "Time Back & Profit" |

## 2. Texas Growth Signals (The Trigger)
*   **TREC Sponsorship Delta:** Scrape TREC for boutique brokers adding new agents (Sponsorship changes).
*   **MLS Production Spikes:** Monitor for a 50%+ spike in listings month-over-month.
*   **Social Media Celebration:** Monitor Instagram and LinkedIn for `#newhire` graphics.
*   **The Outreach:** "Scaling is the hard part—onboarding should be the easy part. Would love to show you the 'Brokerage-in-a-Box' we built for Texas firms."

## 3. The "Association Trojan Horse" (The Scalability Play)
*   **Strategy:** Position Jointly as the **"Brokerage Growth Partner"** for the Association.
*   **The Offer:** Pitch small local AORs (Associations of Realtors) on a "Brokerage Starter Grant" where the Association sponsors the first 3 months of Jointly for any firm under 10 agents.

## 4. The 15-Minute "Speed Demo"
*   **Min 1–5:** **Compliance Dashboard.** One-click approval for TREC standards. (Relief).
*   **Min 5–10:** **Rev-Share Calculator.** Show how 5–10 deals/mo makes the software **free + profitable**. (Profit).
*   **Min 10–15:** **"The 5-Minute Setup."** Show their new "Brokerage-in-a-Box" in action. (Ease).
