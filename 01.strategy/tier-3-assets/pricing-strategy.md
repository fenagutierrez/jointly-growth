# GTM Asset: Pricing Strategy (Texas Tier 3)

## 1. The Tiered Platform Fee (Texas Boutique)
| Tier Name | Size | Platform Fee (Monthly) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Solo/Duo** | 1–3 Agents | **$249/mo** |
| **Boutique** | 4–10 Agents | **$399/mo** |
| **Power Boutique** | 11–20 Agents | **$549/mo** |

## 2. The "Net-Zero" Model (Profitability Lever)
The "Secret Sauce" of this pricing is the **Concierge Rev-Share**. This is how you close the deal when they say, "I already pay for DocuSign."
*   **The Pitch:** *"If your agents run just 4–8 deals a month through our Concierge (Utilities/Insurance/Home Services), your Rev-Share check covers the entire cost of the platform."*
*   **The Result:** Software effectively becomes **free**, and the Broker gets a "Compliance-as-a-Service" department for $0 net cost.

## 3. The "Texas Compliance Setup" Fee
*   **Standard Setup:** $499 (One-time).
    *   Includes: Importing their TREC templates, setting up automated task lists, and a 1-hour team training.
*   **The "High-Velocity" Waiver:** Waive this fee if they sign during the **15-minute Speed Demo** (or within 24 hours). This creates immediate urgency to "Close Now."

## 4. The "Recruiter’s Toolkit" (Value Add)
*   **Narrative:** "A part-time Admin for compliance costs $1,500/mo minimum. Jointly does 90% of that work for 1/5th the price."
*   **Charge agents a "Tech Fee" ($25/mo)** to make the platform **profitable** for the Broker from day one.
