# Seller Lead Generation: Legal Business Models

To provide skip-traced seller leads (e.g., from expired listings) to Jointly clients, the business must be structured according to one of the following legal models to avoid compliance risks and data-brokering liabilities.

## 1. The Official Reseller Agreement
Under this model, Jointly enters into a formal enterprise partnership with a primary data provider (e.g., BatchData).
*   **Mechanism:** Negotiate a White-Label or Reseller Agreement rather than using a standard self-serve API.
*   **The Catch:** Requires strict compliance measures embedded in the Jointly software and typically involves significant monthly minimum commitments (thousands of dollars).
*   **Best For:** Deep, native integration where Jointly controls the entire data experience.

## 2. The "Bring Your Own Key" (BYOK) SaaS Model
In this model, Jointly provides the software infrastructure but does not sell the data itself.
*   **Mechanism:** Create an integration page within Jointly. Clients create their own accounts with the API provider (e.g., BatchData), obtain their own API keys, and input them into Jointly.
*   **The Action:** Jointly's software makes API calls on the client's behalf using *their* credentials.
*   **Revenue:** The client pays Jointly a monthly software fee and pays the data provider directly for skip tracing.
*   **Best For:** Rapid deployment with zero data-brokering liability.

## 3. The Lead Generation Agency Model (Sell the Appointment)
This model shifts the value from "Data" to "Verified Intent."
*   **Mechanism:** Jointly (or a managed partner) buys the data for internal use. A team of Virtual Assistants (VAs) or internal reps call the expired listings.
*   **The Action:** When a homeowner expressions a verified intent to list ("Yes, I am looking to list my house next week"), the warm, qualified appointment is passed to the client.
*   **Revenue:** Jointly charges a premium fee per vetted appointment or collects a referral fee at closing.
*   **Best For:** High-value Tier 1 and Tier 2 accounts looking for a "Done-For-You" growth solution.
