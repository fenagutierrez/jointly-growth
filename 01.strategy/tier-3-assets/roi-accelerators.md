# GTM Asset: ROI Accelerators (The "Sticky" Value Add)

To drive higher retention and show immediate ROI for Tier 3 brokerages, we offer infrastructure upgrades that solve both their "Front-End Visibility" and "Back-End Intake" problems.

## 1. The "Boutique Refresh" (Website Replacement)
Many Tier 3 firms operate on outdated, non-responsive websites that damage their recruiting and client trust.
*   **The Offer:** A modern, high-conversion real estate website powered by **Webflow**.
*   **Includes:** Choice of 3-5 professional templates, with the **Jovio.com template** serving as our high-end baseline.
*   **Infrastructure:** A fully integrated **CMS** for team and listing management.
*   **The Price:** Only **$29/mo** (included or as a pass-through).
*   **The Value:** Dramatically lowers their tech overhead (saving $100-$300/mo vs legacy providers) while providing a national-brand aesthetic.

## 2. Automated Deal Intake (The "Contact-to-Closing" Loop)
We remove the manual entry step for new leads.
*   **Strategy:** Link the new (or existing) website to Jointly via a smart contact form.
*   **The Action:** When a prospect fills out a form (Buy/Sell/Lease), it **automatically creates a Deal in Jointly** with the status "Onboarding."
*   **The Value:** Eliminates lead leakage. The Broker or Agent is immediately prompted to start the compliance/transaction workflow.

## 3. Automated Listing Sync (The "Update-Once" Rule)
Reducing redundant data entry across platforms.
*   **Strategy:** Active deals in Jointly are automatically pushed to the brokerage website's "Featured Listings" section.
*   **The Action:** Once a deal is created and verified in Jointly, the property details, images, and status are synced to the public site.
*   **The Value:** The brokerage site is always up-to-date with "Real Data" from the transaction engine, not manual web updates.

## 4. The "Content Kickstart" (One-Time Media Package)
To ensure the new website looks high-end on day one, we solve the "Empty Template" problem by providing professional content.
*   **The Offer:** Partnership with local media small businesses to provide a high-production "Site Launch" assets.
*   **Includes:** Professional office photography, team headshots, and 1-2 high-quality short background videos.
*   **The Price:** A competitive one-time fee (vendor-managed).
*   **The Value:** Removes the final barrier to launching a professional brand identity. The Broker doesn't have to source a photographer or edit files; the assets are delivered directly into their Webflow CMS.
