# Jointly Business Pricing Structure

This document outlines the tiered pricing model for the "Jointly Business" edition as of March 2026.

## 1. Platform & Seat Tiers

| Item | Range | Rate | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Base Platform Fee** | N/A | **$249.00/mo** | Standard monthly platform access fee. |
| **Tier 1 Seats** | 1 to 5 | **Included** | First 5 seats are covered by the base fee. |
| **Tier 2 Seats** | 6 to 25 | **$25.00/seat** | Up to 20 additional seats. |
| **Tier 3 Seats** | 26 to 49 | **$15.00/seat** | Up to 24 additional seats. |
| **Tier 4 Seats** | 50 to 99 | **$10.00/seat** | Up to 50 additional seats. |
| **Tier 5 Seats** | 100+ | **$5.00/seat** | Unlimited seats above 100. |

---

## 2. Calculation Logic Example (650 Seats)

To calculate the Monthly Total for a firm with 650 agents:

1.  **Base Fee:** $249.00
2.  **Seats 1-5:** $0.00 (Included)
3.  **Seats 6-25 (20 seats):** 20 * $25 = $500.00
4.  **Seats 26-49 (24 seats):** 24 * $15 = $360.00
5.  **Seats 50-99 (50 seats):** 50 * $10 = $500.00
6.  **Seats 100-650 (551 seats):** 551 * $5 = $2,755.00

**Monthly Total:** **$4,364.00**
**Average Cost Per Seat:** ~$6.71/seat
