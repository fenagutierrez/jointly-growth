# Brokerage Profile: Kelly Simon Properties (KSP)
**Research Date:** April 21, 2026
**Status:** High-Signal Prospect

## 1. Executive Summary
Kelly Simon Properties (KSP) is a boutique, high-volume independent brokerage based in Houston, TX. With 37 agents and a heavy emphasis on both high-end sales and consistent lease volume, KSP represents a prime Tier 1 prospect for Jointly. The brokerage operates several successful sub-teams, including the Selling Houston Team and Vital Realty Group, and maintains a polished digital presence. Their significant lease production (144 sides in 12 months) positions them as a target for Jointly's lease automation and concierge services.
