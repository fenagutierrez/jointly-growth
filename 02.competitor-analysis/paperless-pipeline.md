# Competitor Intelligence: Paperless Pipeline

**Source:** Website Analysis & Competitive Research
**Date:** March 23, 2026
**Target Segment:** Boutique Brokerages & High-Producing Teams (Efficiency-focused)

## 1. Paperless Pipeline's Value Proposition (March 2026)
Paperless Pipeline positions itself as a stable, unobtrusive "central hub" for real estate operations. Their primary promise is to provide a reliable system that supports existing workflows without forcing a change in behavior.

*   **Stability & Reliability:** Highlighting 17 years in the industry and millions of transactions. They are the "safe" choice for brokers who fear tech disruptions.
*   **Simplicity over Complexity:** Explicitly branding themselves against "frustrating and overly complicated" systems. They want to be the "quiet" powerhouse in the background.
*   **"Users are Free" Model:** Usage-based pricing (per transaction) instead of per-seat SaaS. This removes friction for recruitment and adding Transaction Coordinators (TCs).

## 2. Pricing & Plans (March 2026)
Paperless Pipeline uses a **production-based pricing model**. All plans include **unlimited users, storage, and locations**. Costs scale based on the number of **new transactions** created each month.

| New Monthly Transactions | Base Cost (Transaction Mgmt) | With Commission Module |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **5** Transactions | $65 | $119 |
| **10** Transactions | $130 | $184 |
| **25** Transactions | $175 | $234 |
| **40** Transactions | $220 | $279 |
| **80** Transactions | $305 | $414 |
| **150** Transactions | $390 | $504 |
| **250** Transactions | $495 | $609 |

*   **Unlimited Plan:** $675/month for up to 450 new transactions; **$1.50** per additional deal.
*   **Commission Module:** An optional add-on (approx. **$54–$114/mo**) for agent statements and production reporting.

## 3. Key Messaging Features
*   **Transaction Management & Compliance:** Checklists, automated dates, and integrated "Pipeline eSign."
*   **Commission Management:** Automating Disbursement Instructions (DAs) and providing financial reporting for brokers.
*   **TC-Centric Design:** Specifically targeting Transaction Coordinators as the primary users who "carry the deals."
*   **Adaptable Workflows:** Messaging around "No Forced Change"—the software adapts to the brokerage's existing process.
