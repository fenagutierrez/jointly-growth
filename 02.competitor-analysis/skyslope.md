# Competitor Intelligence: SkySlope

**Source:** Website Analysis & Market Research
**Date:** April 16, 2026
**Target Segment:** Enterprise Brokerages & Large Teams (Automation-focused)

## 1. SkySlope's Value Proposition (April 2026)
SkySlope is positioning itself as the "AI-First" brokerage management platform. Their current messaging focuses on using "intelligent automation" to fix the friction points in traditional real estate workflows.

*   **"Smart Suite" AI Integration:** A heavy emphasis on AI-driven features like `Auto-Split & Assign` (splitting PDFs), `Smart Assist` (automated auditing), and `Smart Scan` (extracting data from contracts).
*   **Scale & Trust:** Highlighting their massive footprint—900,000+ users and 3 million+ annual transactions. They are the "Industry Standard" incumbent for large-scale firms.
*   **Integrated Ecosystem:** Bundling Transaction Management with SkySlope Forms (including Texas REALTORS®), DigiSign (e-signature), and SkySlope Books (accounting/commissions).

## 2. Key Messaging Features
*   **Auto-Split & Assign:** AI that splits large PDF packets and routes documents to checklist items automatically (claiming a 97% reduction in agent time).
*   **Smart Blocks:** Auto-detecting signature locations to cut envelope prep time in half.
*   **SkySight:** An AI-powered "weekly briefing" podcast for brokers to hear their business stats on the go.
*   **Ayce:** An AI-powered coaching tool for growth-minded agents.
*   **Voice Offers:** Starting agreements hands-free via voice command (currently limited availability).

## 3. Pricing & Plans (April 2026)
SkySlope primarily uses **Enterprise/Custom Pricing**.
*   **Tiered SaaS Model:** Typically billed as a per-agent/per-month fee or a flat monthly brokerage fee based on size.
*   **Bundled Benefits:** Often included in national franchise fees (e.g., eXp Realty) or as a corporate-level mandate.
*   **Add-ons:** "SkySlope Books" and advanced AI features in the "Smart Suite" may carry additional costs or require specific plan tiers.
