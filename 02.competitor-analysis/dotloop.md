# Competitor Intelligence: dotloop

**Source:** Marketing Email "Why top brokerages choose dotloop"
**Date:** March 10, 2026
**Target Segment:** Brokerage Business+ (Growth-focused firms)

## 1. dotloop's Value Proposition (March 2026)
Dotloop is currently positioning itself as the "Brokerage Platform Built for Growth," focusing on three core pillars:

*   **Recruiting & Retention:** Promising "best-in-class technology" to attract top agents. They highlight mobile access and real-time collaboration.
*   **Operational Visibility:** A single dashboard to see all transactions, offices, and agents, with reporting designed for coaching and scaling.
*   **Automated Compliance:** Using automated workflows to standardize and enforce policies across multiple offices and teams.

## 2. Key Messaging Features
*   **Collaborative Space:** Bringing all parties into one digital space.
*   **Mobile App:** Specifically highlighting texting functionality.
*   **Single Sign-On:** Staff efficiency via one login for multiple profiles.
*   **Market Share:** Claiming to support over 7,500 brokerages.

## 3. Jointly Counter-Positioning (Internal Notes)
Based on our [Tier 3 Competitive Analysis](../tier-3-assets/competitive-analysis.md), here is how we win against this messaging:

| dotloop Claim | Jointly Reality / Counter |
| :--- | :--- |
| **"Built for Growth"** | dotloop is a "Legacy Giant." Their UX is often described as clunky and outdated. Jointly is "Mobile-First" and built for the modern agent on-the-go. |
| **"Visibility & Compliance"** | We offer "Compliance-as-a-Service." While they provide a tool to check files, we automate the TREC standards specifically for Texas. |
| **"Technology for Agents"** | dotloop is a "Time-Tax." Jointly's "TurboTax" experience for clients makes the agent look like a tech-powered powerhouse. |
| **"Business Asset"** | **The Killer Blow:** dotloop is a "Bill" (an expense). Jointly is an **Asset** because of our **Concierge Rev-Share** model. We can make the software "Net-Zero" cost. |

## 4. Sales Hook for dotloop Switchers
*"Dotloop helps you see your deals; Jointly helps you pay for them. If your agents run just 4–8 deals a month through our Concierge, your Rev-Share check covers the entire cost of our platform. Why pay for a legacy file-manager when you can have a profit-generating infrastructure?"*
